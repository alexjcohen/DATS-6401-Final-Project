Economic Indicators - Income

Economic indicators are the most likely cause of the increases we see to rent and housing prices bceause of the direct tie to the ability to pay rent and mortgages. We would expect the median income of a city (a proxy for the wealth of the average citizen) to be directly tied to the value of their property, reflected by home value and rent payments.
